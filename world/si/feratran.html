 Feratrans have a human-like structure to them. They primarily walk on their back legs and have opposable thumbs. They’re very tall, though because of their shape-shifting ability they are able to shorten to look like an average human.

Habitat

  Feratrans are found around the world. However, Feratrans originated from Europe, the Middle East, and North Africa. So most likely European imperialism is why other races can have a Feratran population.

Common Behaviors

  “Animalistic” behaviors/impulses are quite common among Feratrans. Though to act on these is extremely frowned upon by society. For example, when a cat is flustered, it’ll start licking itself. This is a reaction/urge Felidae Feratrans may have. Though, for the most part, they are able to resist this. Most Feratrans, upkeep their forms monthly. Upkeeps vary, some salons might offer an upkeep package that’s like 60 dollars, though most Feratrans do it at home. Many Feratrans are attached to animals, they see them as children for the most part.

Family Structure

  Families usually consist of one or two parents and children.
