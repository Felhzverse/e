Feratran



Eyote Name (Romanized)
Feratrau

Scientific Name
Fera comutāta
Native Planet
Earth
Average Size (Male;Female)
86 inches (218.44 cm), 69 inches (175.26 cm); 81 inches (205.74 cm) 64 inches (162.56 cm)
Average Lifespan
Diet Type
Omnivorous

Feratrans have a human-like structure to them. They primarily walk on their back legs and have opposable thumbs. They’re very tall, though because of their shape-shifting ability they are able to shorten to look average human. Their stature is shortened by 1’5” feet exactly. A Feratran’s hair may appear to look human but it has the ability to stick up, when disturbed/scared/or trying to appear more threatening (of course longer hair would just raise as much as it could before gravity would pull it down). Hair color and fur color can differ from each other. Sometimes facial marking on their Fera form can translate to their face when they’re human.
