 Zelski are a lot shorter than humans! This is how they stay compact and functional. They have catlike ears atop their heads that can go into folds of skin atop their heads, and they are humanoids.

Types

 

Habitat

Zelski quexmozh-die'xen, or religious communities, like to live in the forest, isolated from humanity and interaction, with the food source of the animals around. Zelski who do not live in these religious communities, tend to live under the radar, and are seen as loners. But overall, these loners live what is closest to a human life.

Language

  Zelski are taught the language of their region from birth, but usually are also taught Kezian, the holy language to Kezianism. It is meant only to be spoken to other Kezian Zelski, and is used in prayer or in the preacher’s building more than anywhere else. Most religious things are taught in only Kezian, but in a more casual setting, a Zelski would just speak the language of their location.

Way of Dress

 The Zeski way of dress, when not in a religious situation, is pretty similar to the average human of the place they live in, with a few differences. At night, both girls and boys wear nightgowns. During the day, Zelski wear normal human clothing.
 A way to show peace is to wear a dress. The Zelski believe that a dress is not suitable fighting attire, so when a dress is worn (regardless of gender) it is a sign of peace and diplomacy. Clothing in religious setting, shows rank.

  • Apprentice preacher: 1 thin band on each arm, 3 black dots on each band
  • Preacher: 3 thin bands on each arm. 1 black dot on each
  • Leader: 6 thin black bands on right arm, one dot on each
  • Keznivizdan: 6 thin black bands on left arm, one dot on each
    •  Men and women are equal and have the same standards. Defenders do not wear arm bands, but they wear wrist bands and instead of the preachers dresses, they wear rompers of the same style, with black leggings underneath, and sturdy black steel toed boots.
