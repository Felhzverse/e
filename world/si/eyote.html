Diet Type
Carnivorous, regressively Omnivorous

The dominant species on the planet of Yeoliv, sharing some biological similarities with humanoid species of Earth. Eyote are most easily recognized in the slash-like marks covering their skin caused by irregular binding of melanin, which become more prominent and multuous with age, in addition to their being noticeably taller than humans on average. Their only other noticeable physical trait is being excessively hair, on account of the cold climate of Yeoliv.
