Don't Take it Seriously is a partially slice of life, partially comedic comic thought up and drawn by Amir , and also written/read over by Christine. It's about Bluzie Retsbew, an upcoming eighth grader who's reluctant on growing up and having to face new challenges and responsibilities. Her response? Refuse to grow up. Her parents and friends, on the other hand, aren't very supportive of this idea. This is a project that's been slowly improved and developed upon since I(Amir) came up with it in 2013, and I really hope you enjoy it!
