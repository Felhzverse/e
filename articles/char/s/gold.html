Personality


He’s pretty calm, cool and collected for the most part. He’s witty and likes to joke around, but is a straight edge rule-follower. Gold will never do anything that will break a rule, especially if it’s religious, unless he is forced to. He will go the extra mile to impress friends and family, and is a teacher’s pet.

Appearance


He’s average weight, with curly brown hair, like his sister’s. But he’s always seen with straight hair. He commits a lot of time to blow drying it straight. He has green eyes and freckles. He has a tear in his left ear, due to an accident with a thorn bush.

Story


He was the first child born to Stride and Raspberry. He watches his parents, and how they fight constantly, and tries to ignore it. He blames himself for getting involved, even if he ends up getting hurt. He struggles with manic depression, but since this is taboo, he tries not to show it.

Way of Dress


He’s seen wearing religious clothes more often than not! Even when he’s not praying, he wears necklaces and bracelets and even sometimes the entire outfit. Though, when in normal situations like school he wears mainly t-shirts and jeans. He wears the same black boots, every day.
