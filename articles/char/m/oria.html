Personality

  Generally, she’s a quiet person but will open up more to people as she spends time with them. She doesn’t like people very much so she usually keeps her distance from them.

Appearance

 White, long, wavy brown hair, but dyed aquamarine and mostly worn straight, blue eyes, relatively thin build.

Story

 Her family is from Italy, but she was brought up in London, England during her childhood. School was tough as she struggled to make any friends and tended to stand out from others, which led to some others starting to avoid her. After completing college, she moved to Canberra, Australia and lives there currently.

Way of Dress

 Anything in terms of clothing, really, but she always wears a hoodie. Hoodies are usually a dark colour, mostly dark blue or black. She always wears a rough amethyst crystal around her neck, and nothing else.
