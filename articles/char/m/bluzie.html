Appearance


Bluzie is a thin lanky Eyote kid. She has medium-brown skin with dark brown eyes and hair. Her hair is type 4a (very curly), and although normally in a ponytail, comes down a bit below her shoulders when fully out. She can usually be seen with the colors green, blue, or pink somewhere in her outfit.

Bluzie is a pretty casual person. For the most part, she floats through life in a baggy shirt and sweatpants with sneakers, unless asked to do otherwise.
