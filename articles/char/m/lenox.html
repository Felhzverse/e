 Lenox is the CEO of the Felhzverse Corporation for the betterment of the universe. They’re a pretty no-nonsense person, and although they can be strict and harsh at times, at the end of the day they mean well.

Personality

 Lenox tends to be more reserved and stoic on the outside. They try to live in the most orderly way possible to limit stress, and don’t like having beef with anyone. When they have a goal they’ll go by almost any means to get it done, and like to stay active.

Appearance

 Lenox is a tall Cai eyote who is of average build, although on the thinner side. They have medium brown skin, darker brown eyes, and near-black hair, which is often worn in a bun or ponytail.

Story

  Lenox started off as a relatively sheltered wide-eyed kid who went into international law thinking that they’d change the world and make lives better. Things didn’t turn out that way, but they’re trying to make the most of their situation.

Way of Dress

 Lenox usually wears sweater vests with sneakers. This isn’t the only thing they wear, but expect to see this getup in every photo they’re in. I think they forgot their other clothes existed and just didn’t bother to look through the rest of their wardrobe.
