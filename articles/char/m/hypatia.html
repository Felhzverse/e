Personality


Hypatia is very loud and proud of who she is and very supportive to those around her. She often is cheerful and loves to try and spread this positivity to everyone she can. She’s very laid back and understanding of others, however if she feels very strongly on an opinion she will not back down and it is often hard to change her mind once it’s set. Sometimes she can be a bit overbearing and have a hard time shutting up and listening. For the most part she’s an open book, unafraid to show her emotions. However it is not unheard of for her to close off. She often blames herself for misfortunes.

Appearance


Hypatia is over a foot taller than average human women and heavy set. She has thick, curly, brown hair that reaches to just below her ribs. She has a prominent nose, thick eyebrows, olive skin, and dark cobalt blue eyes, often behind a pair of round, purple glasses.

Story


Hypatia has always lived a very comfortable life, always had friends and a supportive family. She chased her dreams of becoming a painter, moving with her best friend, Nadzieja, into a small house in San Francisco. The two urged each other on with their respective passions, helping each other reach greater heights.

Way of Dress


Hypatia is a very laid back and chill person. Her sense of fashion also reflects this with her often wearing whatever is most comfortable. She mostly wears loose fitting clothes and sneakers.
