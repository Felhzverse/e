Way of Dress


Different dresses in quite a strange way. He loves ugly, tacky sweaters, and wears winged eyeliner all of the time. He really doesn’t care what people think of him. He just wears what he thinks is cool. He also wears a lot of band T-Shirts, and mainly either wears baggy sweatpants or shorts that are probably too short. Different’s strange obsession with jewelry is an enigma. He always has at least 3 gold bangles on his right wrist, and he wears at least 3 rings at a time, sometimes more than one on each finger. He also wears gold bangle style necklaces a lot. He also, if in the mood will wear boots with wedge or square heels. But, for the most part, doesn’t put that much effort into shoe choice and tends to wear the same sneakers a lot.
