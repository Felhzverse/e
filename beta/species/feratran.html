 Feratrans are descendants of humans who were cursed. They have the ability to shapeshift.

Habitat

 Feratrans are able to live anywhere suitable for human life. Though in the end where they choose to live depends on what is accessible and personal preference.

Common Behaviors

  Feratrans can display behaviors associated with animals. Such as Feline Feratrans may clean themselves as a self-soothing mechanism, or they can be interested in playing with string or other cat toys. Feratrans behavior differ from Feratran to Feratran. Also culture has an impact on Feratrans beliefs and behavior.

Family Structure

  Note- Magical puberty differs from physical puberty. Magical puberty can start as early as 6 and end late as 23. During ages 6-10, are Feratrans gain their shapeshifting abilities, which then mature until approximately age 23. Their animal forms don’t age as fast as a real animals. They age in accordance to how old the animal would be in human years.
Family structures depend on the Feratran. Culture may be a factor or sexual preference. Families can include:
- Single parent
- Partner/Partner
- Guardian/Guardian
- Amount of children is up to the parents/guardians.

Life Stages:

  • Infant: 0-1 years

  • Toddler: 2-3 years

  • Child: 4-9 years

  • Puberty: 10-13 years

  • Adolescent: 14-18 years

  • Young adult: 19-30 years

  • Middle aged: 31-65 years

  • Senior: 66+ years

Diet

 Most Feratrans eat the same types of food at the same frequency as humans. If a Feratran chooses to live primarily in their Fera form, they will eat more than they would normally in human form due to their larger size. They are omnivores by default.

Way of Dress

  Their clothes can be enchanted so that, they disappear or change size accordingly with a Feratran’s changing form. Clothes may also be changed to make a tail hole if the Feratran is in their Fera-form or Anthro-form. Feratrans follow the same clothing trends as other humanoids on Earth.

Religion

  A Feratran’s spiritual beliefs will vary. There are Feratrans who follow all religions. Though worshiped/idolized Feratrans within their culture are called Saunties. One Feratran made religion is Sauntism. Sauntism is not practiced among all Feratrans, it has a small following.
