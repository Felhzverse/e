Felhzverse

 Written and drawn by Alice, Life’s Expectations is about how much can a teen bluff without breaking Joking aside, Life’s Expectation is a coming of age story that deals with topics such as homophobia, and racism (and a lot more). Though, LE is more of a comedic-slice of life take on teen life. Bubs (the main character and kinda antagonist?) is a Feratran (another species of human, that will be gone into depth in the story). Bubs character development relies heavily on the consequences of her actions, which is what a lot of teens come to learn through life experiences. I kinda made Bubs her own villain because that’s something struggle with a lot, I really hope you enjoy Life’s Expectations, I’ve been working on it since 2014.
 Felhzverse™ is run by a group of friends who love to create stories. We only take credit for creating the universe, and helping it grow. If you have any questions, comments, or concerns, please contact us on our email or discord! We will try to respond as soon as possible.
