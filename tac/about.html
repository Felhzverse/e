 "The Adventure Crew" written and drawn by Kienen The Adventure Crew is a story about a group of kids picked to figure out what the world has become after an apocalypse that had sent Earth into chaos. It's a story about a journey of self discovery while trying to also solve the mystery of what lurks in the forest. These characters are relatively new in my roster of characters, and I’m excited to finally build up their story!
 Felhzverse™ is run by a group of friends who love to create stories. We only take credit for creating the universe, and helping it grow. If you have any questions, comments, or concerns, please contact us on our email or discord! We will try to respond as soon as possible.
